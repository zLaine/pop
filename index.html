Medic

  • High Concept: Players perform triage as a conscientious objector during the war.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players will have to decide whether they have time to treat all a patient's wounds or pass them by -- you only have so much time to spend per person when doing triage.
  • Genre: A time-based medical sim game.
  • Game play: The player uses the mouse to drag banadages to the wounds on the soldier to patch them up.
  • Prototype goal: Experimentation with making a time driven game that is fun instead of frustrating/stressful.
  • Features: The concept is somewhat unique, but the more interesting gameplay mechanics haven't been added in yet, as this is more of a test on working with time sensitive games -- an aspect of gameplay I generally dislike as a player.
  • Setting/Premise: It's about a soldier drafted for the Vietnam war who dislikes war and killing -- so he opts to become a medic instead.
  • Target Audience: People who have an intest in emergenct medical situations (as the later mechanics do actually reflect real-life priorities when in a triage situation) and making life or death choices.
  • Play time: About a minute, can get longer as you get better at the game.
  • Strategies: Effective strategies in the current version bandaging those with 3 or less injuries -- more and you should pass.
